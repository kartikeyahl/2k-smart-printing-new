2K Smart Printing


In this busy world, we understand how precious time can be for our user’s and we observed that they feel a lot of problems in their daily life. One of the problems raised is managing time to stand in a queue in printing shops to print required documents. As a student our team personally faced this issue while there is a placement drive in the morning and you are informed just a night before. Now, in that case it's obvious that many of us will rush in the morning to get our Resume and CV printed and observing similar issues in every organization we come with a solution to this, that is “2K Smart Printing”. Now users can give commands to print online through this extension and pay the money. Now all they need is to go and pick their printed documents with them. This is secure and feasible as no data will be saved, payment is encrypted and the user can only collect their documents by verification method. The shopkeeper will only give documents to you if you have the auto generated pin with you for your documents.
